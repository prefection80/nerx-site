How It Works

How buying works

  1. Click your session's date on the calendar above — that's how you first reserve and pay for a session. Each date shows its own price and seats remaining.
  2. Pay with PayPal right there — secure checkout in the popup. Signing up for several trainings, or buying for your team? Use Add to Cart to collect seats across any sessions, name the attendee for each seat (or leave it blank for yourself), and check out once. You get a receipt for the full order plus a separate email listing every ticket code and who it's for, and each named attendee is emailed their own code. If your PayPal account payment has issues, try paying with a debit or credit card instead (no PayPal account needed).
  3. Done — after the payment is processed, your ticket code is sent to the email address you submitted at checkout (usually within ~2 minutes) and your seat is reserved automatically. Your ticket also lets you change to a different session later. Buying both parts of a two-part course means two purchases (two tickets).
  4. At class time — return to this page, enter your code, and your "Join Training" button appears one hour before the session. Need a different date later? Your code lets you reschedule up to one hour before the session starts.
