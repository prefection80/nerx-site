One-Line Development & Equipment Sizing

The cheapest time to fix an electrical design problem is before anything is purchased. NERX supports project scoping with system configuration studies, one-line development, and equipment sizing that reflect how the system will actually be operated and protected.

We develop the protection strategy alongside the one-line — so breaker ratings, CT selections, and relay schemes are consistent from the first drawing rather than reconciled after procurement.

Typical Scope & Deliverables

  • Conceptual and detailed one-line diagrams
  • Equipment sizing and ratings
  • System configuration alternatives
  • Protection strategy development
  • Procurement specification support
