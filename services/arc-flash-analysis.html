Arc Flash Analysis & Mitigation

An arc flash study that ends with a label on the panel is only half the job. The real objective is reducing the incident energy your personnel are exposed to — so that maintenance and troubleshooting can be performed safely, in practical PPE, without shutting the facility down.

NERX performs arc flash evaluations in accordance with IEEE 1584 and NFPA 70E, then goes further: we identify where incident energy can be reduced through protective device settings changes, maintenance-mode switching, relay upgrades, or system reconfiguration — and we quantify the improvement each option buys so you can prioritize investment.

Typical Scope & Deliverables

  • Arc flash incident energy study (IEEE 1584 / NFPA 70E)
  • Equipment labeling data
  • Mitigation alternatives with quantified incident-energy reductions
  • Protective device settings recommendations
  • Electrical safety program support
