Protection System Misoperation Investigations

Did your protection system operate when it shouldn't have — or fail to operate when it should? Misoperations are more than an operational headache: for NERC registered entities they can trigger PRC-004 investigation and reporting obligations with firm deadlines.

NERX investigates unexpected operations end to end: event record and relay target analysis, settings review, root-cause determination, and corrective action planning. Where PRC-004 applies, we prepare the documentation needed to fulfill the requirement.

Typical Scope & Deliverables

  • Event record, DFR, and relay target analysis
  • Root-cause determination
  • NERC PRC-004 investigation and reporting support
  • Corrective action plans
  • Protection settings review
