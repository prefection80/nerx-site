Protective Relaying & Testing

Protection is where power system engineering meets consequence. Settings that are wrong in either direction cost you — equipment damage and safety exposure on one side, nuisance trips and lost production on the other.

NERX designs, sets, implements, and maintains protective relaying systems. We develop coordinated settings, support commissioning and testing, and validate that as-left settings match approved values for compliance purposes.

Typical Scope & Deliverables

  • Relay settings development
  • Coordination studies
  • Protection scheme design and upgrades
  • Commissioning and testing support
  • Settings validation and documentation (including PRC-005 support)
