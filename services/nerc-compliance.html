NERC Compliance Programs

Compliance programs fail in two directions: too little rigor, and meaningless box checking that consumes staff time without making the system more reliable. NERX builds programs aimed at both audit defensibility and genuine reliability value.

We provide training, procedure development, oversight systems, and internal auditing — tailored to your registration and functions, with practical evidence practices your engineers can actually sustain.

Typical Scope & Deliverables

  • Compliance program and procedure development
  • Internal and mock audits
  • Compliance training
  • PRC, MOD, and FAC standard support
  • Audit evidence preparation
