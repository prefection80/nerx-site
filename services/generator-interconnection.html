Generator Interconnection Coordination

Interconnecting generation to the grid is a long process with many handoffs — application, study phases, facility design, and ongoing modeling obligations after commercial operation. Missed or inaccurate data submittals cost time and money.

NERX guides clients through the interconnection process and ensures modeling information reaches the regional planning bodies accurately and on time, from the initial request through ongoing data obligations under the NERC MOD standards.

Typical Scope & Deliverables

  • Interconnection application and study-phase support
  • Model preparation and submittal
  • Coordination with utilities and regional planning bodies
  • MOD-series compliance data support
  • Protection and facility design coordination
